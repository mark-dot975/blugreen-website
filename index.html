How to Test Egg Freshness Using Water

A simple water test to determine egg freshness and safety:

  • Fresh and Safe to eat: Egg sinks to bottom
  • Too old to eat but not fresh: Egg stands on point
  • Not safe to be eaten: Egg floats to top

This simple method helps reduce food waste and ensures food safety.
